Twisty Bloom Catalogue

Project Overview

The Twisty Bloom Catalogue is a responsive e-commerce website built using Wix that specializes in selling flower bouquets and potted flowers for various occasions such as birthdays, parties, and gifts. The platform offers an elegant design, user-friendly navigation, and an integrated checkout and payment system, ensuring a seamless shopping experience for customers.

Key Features

  • Product Catalog: Comprehensive listing of flower bouquets and potted plants.Detailed product descriptions, including type, design, and recommended occasions and High-quality images showcasing each product's design.
  • Occasion-Based Categories: A "Best Sellers" and "New Arrivals" section for quick browsing.
  • Shopping and Checkout: Cart functionality allowing users to add, remove, and modify items. Secure and user-friendly checkout process.
