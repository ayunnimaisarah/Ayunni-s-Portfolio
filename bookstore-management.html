Bookstore Management System

Project Overview

The Bookstore Management System was developed to streamline operations within a bookstore, including inventory control, customer loyalty programs, and sales processing. The project aimed to improve efficiency, reduce manual errors, and enhance the overall customer experience.

Key Features

  • Book Inventory Management: Tracks book titles, unique IDs, prices, and conditions (new/preloved).
  • Membership Management: Automatically applies discounts for members during checkout.
  • Sales and Discount Processing: Discounts calculated dynamically for members.
  • Customer-Friendly Interface: Simplifies entering travel details and viewing available options.

Role and Contributions

  • Implemented core backend functionalities, including book inventory management, membership discounts, and sales processing..
  • ntegrated database models to store and retrieve book and customer information efficiently.

Challenges Faced and Solutions

  • Challenges: Maintaining consistency between new and preloved book inventory, given their distinct pricing and conditions.
  • Solution: Created separate database models for book conditions while using a unified search and reporting mechanism.
  • Challenges:Implementing dynamic discounts for members without slowing down the checkout process.
  • Solution: Pre-computed discount values for common scenarios and optimized the database queries to minimize response time.
