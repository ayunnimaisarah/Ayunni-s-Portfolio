Hair Care Application

Project Overview

The Hair Care Application is a mobile app designed using MIT App Inventor to provide users with a seamless experience for buying hair care products and accessing personalized tips for maintaining healthy hair. The app aims to simplify online shopping while delivering value-added services, such as expert advice on topics like reducing hair loss, achieving shiny hair, and other hair care routines.

Key Features

  • Product Catalog: Displays a variety of hair care products with images, descriptions, and prices. Organized into categories for easy navigation example shampoos, conditioners, oils.
  • Hair Care Tips: Provides articles and tips on hair care, covering topics such as preventing hair loss, maintaining shiny strong hair and fizzy hair
  • Cart and Checkout: Allows users to add products to a shopping cart. Includes a secure checkout process with payment integration.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple drag-and-drop design powered by MIT App Inventor. Easy-to-navigate UI with intuitive controls for non-technical users.

Role and Contributions

  • Designed the app layout and user interface using MIT App Inventor.
  • Implemented the product catalog and integrated a dynamic filtering option for product categories.
  • Developed the hair care tips module, writing engaging content and ensuring easy updates via backend integration.
    • Programmed the cart and checkout system, including item addition, removal, and price calculation.

Challenges Faced and Solutions

  • Challenges: Designing an appealing UI within MIT App Inventor's constraints.
  • Solution: Leveraged creative layouts, color schemes, and icons to make the app visually appealing and user-friendly.
  • Challenges: Handling cart logic, such as maintaining the cart state after the app was closed.
  • Solution: Implemented persistent data storage using TinyDB in MIT App Inventor to save cart information locally on the device.
